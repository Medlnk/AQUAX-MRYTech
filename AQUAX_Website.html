About AQUA-X

AQUA-X is an AI-enhanced autonomous smart buoy for continuous water quality monitoring in marine, coastal, and inland water environments.

The system uses a MindSpore 2.8.0 Autoencoder trained on normal water quality readings. At inference, anomalous readings produce high reconstruction error, triggering real-time pollution alerts.

Deployed via Huawei Cloud (ModelArts), the system is fully autonomous — powered by solar panels and rechargeable battery, transmitting data over 4G/5G/LoRa.
